Press the keys:                                                                                                                                                                 Translate the camera:
't' or 'T': see the ray tracing result.                  'n': change the scene.                                                                                  ←: eye move left                →: eye move right
'l': Turn on/off the head light.     'k': Turn on/off the light on the camera.                                                                           ↑: eye move upward           ↓: eye move downward
'p': Aliasing/Anti-Aliasing.         'o': material/no material.         'm': shadow/no shadow.                                                    +: eye move forward            -: eye move backward
'a': lookat anticlockwise           'd': lookat lockwise           'w': lookat upward           's': lookat downward
